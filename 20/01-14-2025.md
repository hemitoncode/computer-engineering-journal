## The I2C 

### Introduction:
Today, I hooked up a LCD display on my project. Usually, there are MANY connections that must be wired. However, with the magic from the I2C module, you can drastically minimize the amount of pins needed by the Arduino.

It works by wiring 2 pins: SDA and SCL to analog pins A4 and A5 respectively. Afterwards, you must use a special library to interact with the LCD.
